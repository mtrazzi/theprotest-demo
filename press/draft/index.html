AI Coalition to March on Google, OpenAI and Anthropic, Demand Halt to AI Development Without Public Consent

Media Contact: Michael Ceraso · michael@yourwinningmargins.org

Stop the AI Race march, San Francisco, March 21, 2026. Photo by Rachel Shu.

What

Stop the AI Race, QuitGPT, Food & Water Watch, the National Union of Healthcare Workers, Stop Gilroy Datacenter Now and AI Action will gather at 1455 3rd St. in San Francisco on Saturday, July 11, before marching to Google, OpenAI and Anthropic to demand the companies halt development of more advanced AI without public consent.

The coalition represents a growing campaign to curb corporate power and stop companies from developing increasingly powerful AI without meaningful public oversight and accountability.

When
Saturday, July 11, noon–3 p.m.
Where
1455 3rd St., San Francisco
Who
Stop the AI Race, QuitGPT, Food & Water Watch, the National Union of Healthcare Workers, Stop Gilroy Datacenter Now and AI Action.
Visuals
Protesters carrying signs and banners marching to Google, OpenAI and Anthropic, stopping outside each company for speeches and chants.
